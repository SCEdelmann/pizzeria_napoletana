Our History

Amici, welcome to the PIZZERIA Napoletana.

We serve you the most delicious Napoletan pizza, a selection of antipasti, salads & desserts and delicious Italian wines.

The dough for our Pizza Napoletana is traditionally made by hand according to a family recipe. It ferments for 48h and is topped with the finest and freshest ingredients and baked in an organic stone oven at 400 degrees lightning fast.

Italian flour, Gustarosso tomato - which reminds us of childhood and is grown in the countryside of Salerno, Fior di Latte d`Agerola - also called flower of milk, handmade Mozzarella di Bufala Campagna DOP- from controlled DOP buffalo breeding of the border region in the north of Caserta, as well as the rest of all dairy products and cured meats are controlled and delivered to us directly from Italy twice a week like the fresh Friarelli, date tomatoes and the rest of the vegetables.

We believe in good ingredients and careful processing.

Our credo: quality, because then it tastes really good. Gusto è Qualità !

Order, pick up or have delivered! From now on our terrace is also open.

Please reserve a table in advance and remember to bring a negative test certificate.

We look forward to seeing you.
